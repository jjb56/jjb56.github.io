About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Jeremy Barnes, and I'm a software developer with an Associate of Science in Computer Programming and a growing background in IT support and technical troubleshooting. This collection of projects represents my development journey, from early coursework and bootcamp projects to independent applications and commercial game development.

Whether you're a recruiter, hiring manager, fellow developer, or simply curious about my work, I hope these projects provide insight into both my technical abilities and my approach to learning and problem solving.
